🎯 TO ADD YOUR LOGO:

1. Prepare your logo image file (PNG, JPG, or JPEG format)

2. Rename it to exactly one of these:
   • company-logo.png   (recommended)
   • company-logo.jpg
   • company-logo.jpeg

3. Place the file in THIS folder (attached_assets/)

4. Restart your server

5. Download a quotation PDF to see your logo!
